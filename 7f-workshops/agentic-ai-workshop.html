The Biggest AI Mistake You Can Make

Foundation

Most coaches use AI to solve problems that don't exist. They build elaborate workflows for things that weren't a bottleneck. They spend 10 hours automating a task that took 20 minutes a week. That's not leverage — that's a new job.

⚠ Signs You're In a Rabbit Hole
You've spent more time building AI systems than actually using them in your business
You're automating tasks you could hire a $10/hr VA to do
You've collected 6+ AI tools but have a consistent workflow in zero of them
You're building an AI system before you've validated what your actual constraint is
✓ The Right Application
Constraint first. What is the single bottleneck slowing your growth right now?
Time audit. Where are you personally spending hours on tasks AI can handle at 80% quality?
Leverage math. If AI frees 10 hrs/week at $500/hr value = $260K/yr in recovered capacity
Build one system, use it daily before adding the next one
The Rule
AI should optimize a real constraint, replace real overhead, or multiply real output. If it doesn't do one of those three things — it's a hobby, not a strategy.
