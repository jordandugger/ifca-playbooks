Step 3 — Weekly Utilization Workflow

This is how to make the dashboard part of your actual weekly ad management routine — not just a one-time exercise.
MON
Weekly Audit — 15 Minutes
Every Monday, open the dashboard and update all active entities with the past 7 days of data. Review the kill rule flags. Any red = cut before noon. Note which entities graduated from yellow to green — those are your scaling candidates.
WED
New Entity Check-In
Any ads launched in the past 48–72 hours that have spent 2× your target KPI threshold? If they haven't hit your hook rate benchmark, kill them now. New ads don't get extra runway — the Andromeda algorithm forms opinions fast.
FRI
Creative Brief — What to Launch Next Week
Look at your top 2–3 green performers. What format, hook type, and angle are they? That's your creative brief for next week. Build 2–3 new variations that swap the hook or shift the format while preserving the winning angle. Add them to the dashboard when they launch.
