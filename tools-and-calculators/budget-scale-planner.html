Launch 50 / Run 10 Plan
🚀 The Ashton Shanks "Launch 50, Run 10" System
The most underused hack in this space. Launch all planned test ads at once — turn most off immediately — turn them on one-by-one as losers die. Zero learning phase resets.

Pre-loaded queue
01Pre-produce — distinct ads across all your entity buckets before launch day.
02Launch all of them in one batch. Immediately turn off — of them — leave only — running.
03When a running ad drops below threshold (2× KPI with no result), turn it off. Turn on one standby ad from your queue — no learning phase reset.
04Replenish the standby queue with — new ads per week. Treat it like inventory — never let standby hit zero.
Weekly Creative Cadence
Monday Pull last week's data. Flag any ad that hit 2× KPI with no booked call. Kill it and activate a standby. Review
Mon–Tue Brief and script — new entity concepts. Assign format bucket, angle, and hook type before filming. Produce
Wed–Thu Film, edit, caption. Batch produce all — new ads in one shoot day. No individual ad should take more than 30 minutes of editing. Film
Thursday Launch — new ads as standbys (off). Activate — to run. Maintain your active ad target. Launch
Friday Check 72-hour hook rates on anything launched Tuesday or earlier. If hook rate < 15% — kill immediately. Don't wait for 2× KPI on algorithm-penalized ads. Kill Check
⚠ Never Edit a Live Winning Ad

Editing resets the ad ID and wipes accumulated micro-learning. Duplicate the winner → edit the duplicate → let the original run untouched. Even one field change kills the learning history.
