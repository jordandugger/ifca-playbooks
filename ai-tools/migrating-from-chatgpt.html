Moving Your Workflows

A practical 4-step migration that takes under an hour and sets you up for faster output than you ever got from ChatGPT.

1

Go to Projects â New Project. Copy your ChatGPT Custom Instructions into the System Prompt. Expand them â add your offer name, target client, typical transformation, tone guide, and words you never use. This is better than Custom Instructions because it's per-project, not global.

Inside your Project, upload: your sales page PDF, your best email sequence, your latest lead magnet, and any brand voice guidelines. These live in the Project permanently. Claude will reference them automatically without you pasting them.
