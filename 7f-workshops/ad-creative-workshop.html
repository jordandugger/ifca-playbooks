Holistic Ad Strategy — The Three-Layer Stack

Section 05

"One funnel isn't a strategy. It's a ceiling." Almost everyone here is running TOF DM lead magnet only. That's what Alycia was doing when she hit her Q4 wall. The warm audience you're paying to build every month is sitting there unmonetized. This is the fix.

TOF · Cold Traffic
Build the Audience
Follower / Profile Visit Ad — best organic content promoted. Builds the MOF/BOF audience you'll convert. $1–2/follower target.

DM Lead Magnet — keyword-triggered freebie, email capture, starts conversation. $5–10 CPL target. This is the engine.

Educational Content Ad — identity, pain, or bold perspective. Not an offer. Builds trust with cold audiences at scale.
60–70% of Budget
MOF · Warm Audience
Warm the Pipeline
Lead Magnet Retarget — same offer, warmer audience. Run 50/50 cold + warm split. Higher-intent converts differently.

Belief-Breaking Testimonial — addresses "I've tried everything" with social proof + mechanism. Followers, video viewers, profile visitors.

Engagement Retarget ($5–10/day) — anyone who engaged in last 30–90 days. Dramatically shortens cash conversion cycle.
15–20% of Budget
BOF · Hot Audience
Convert the Ready
Short-Form VSL → Booking Page — ad acts as the pitch. Cold lead to sale in 48hrs when niche is specific enough.

Direct Offer DM Ad — warm-only. Direct invitation. Only works when trust is already established from TOF/MOF exposure.

Mechanism Testimonial — client explains WHY it worked, not just that it worked. This is what converts skeptics.
15–20% of Budget
Key insight: TOF builds the audience. BOF monetizes it. If you're only running TOF, you're generating warm leads every month and leaving them on the table.

Winning Variation Framework — Iterate Without Killing What's Working

Section 07
Read the Ad Before You Touch It — 4 Questions First
1
What format is it?
What delivery mechanism is earning the media buy? Protect it when you iterate.
2
What emotion does the hook trigger?
That's the real reason it's winning. Protect that emotion in every variation.
3
What funnel stage is responding?
TOF cold vs. MOF retarget — don't mix signals. Same creative to both dilutes both.
4
Where does hold rate drop off?
That tells you what to change vs. what to protect. High hook rate + low hold = fix the body, not the hook.

"If hook rate is 35% but hold rate is 12% — don't change the hook. The problem is in the body. Most coaches change the wrong variable and kill the winner."
