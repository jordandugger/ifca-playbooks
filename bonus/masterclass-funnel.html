How to Set Up Your Masterclass Claude Project
Follow these 5 steps in order. Takes about 15 minutes to set up. After that you have a personalized AI masterclass strategist — not a generic chatbot.
1
Create a New Claude Project
Go to Claude.ai → click Projects in the left sidebar → New Project → name it something like "[Your Name] Masterclass Funnel". A Claude project gives you a persistent workspace where all your files and instructions live together. Every chat inside the project has access to your uploaded docs and system instructions automatically.
→ Open Claude.ai
2
Copy & Paste the Project Instructions
Click the button below to copy the full project instructions, then: inside your Claude project → click the gear icon (Project Settings) → paste into the "Project Instructions" field → save. This tells Claude exactly what frameworks it knows, what role it plays, and how to guide you through each step of the 90-Minute Money Machine system.
ℹ️
You can also click the Project Instructions tab above to read the full text before copying. It includes the complete 7-step system, all KPI benchmarks, and 8 prompt starters.
3
Upload Your Project Files
Inside your Claude project → click Add Content (or the upload icon) → upload your files to the project knowledge base. The more context Claude has about your specific offer and past results, the more targeted its outputs will be. See the File List tab for exactly what to upload — broken down by required, recommended, and already-provided IFCA files.
4
Run the Projection Calculator Inside Claude
Open a new chat inside your Masterclass project. Click the Calculator tab above, then click "Copy JSX Code". Paste the code into the chat and say: "Render this as a live artifact." Claude will display the full interactive calculator — with projection sliders, KPI grading, campaign history, and benchmark reference — as a live tool inside your chat. You can re-open it any time.
⚡
Important: Open the chat from inside your Masterclass project (not a regular chat) so the calculator has access to your project files and context.
5
Start with a Prompt Starter
You don't need to write a long prompt. Click the Prompt Starters tab above to copy any of the 8 ready-to-use prompts. The best first prompt is usually the offer creation one — once Claude knows your avatar, mechanism, and outcome, every other output gets dramatically more specific.
The 7-Step System at a Glance
Every prompt, template, and calculator in this file maps to one of these 7 steps.
Step 1
Offer Creation — Rule of 1 Formula
One avatar. One problem. One mechanism. One outcome. One masterclass title.
Step 2
Funnel & Tech Setup — 2-Hour Build
Landing page → confirmation page → booking/application page. Automation sequences.
Step 3
Creative Development — 14-Day Calendar
Carousels, B-roll reels, talking head ads. DM automation trigger word. Organic + paid flywheel.
Step 4
Setting Projections — KPI Framework
Set CPL, show rate, booking rate, close rate, and ROAS targets before spending $1.
Step 5
Building the Presentation — 90-Min Structure
Intro → 3 Secrets → Client Journey → Safeguards → No-Pressure Pitch. 45–60 min before offer reveal.
Step 6
Hosting Strategy — Performance Tips
Live beats replay every time. Re-run strategy on Sunday. Open loops, engagement, energy.
Step 7
Follow-Up Campaign — 7-Touch Sequence
Post-masterclass within 15 min. Replay → Urgency → Objection → Re-run → Story → DM outreach.
The One Rule
Do not scale ad spend past Campaign #1 until you hit 3× ROAS minimum. Fix your lowest-graded KPI first. Every time.

Step 1–3 — Build the Funnel
Step 1 · Offer CreationHelp me build my Rule of 1 offer. My niche is [describe your avatar and #1 problem]. Pull from the 90-Minute Money Machine framework and give me: 1 specific avatar, 1 big problem, 1 unique mechanism name, 1 clear outcome with timeline, and 5 masterclass title options.
Step 2 · Landing PageWrite my masterclass landing page copy. My offer is [mechanism name] for [avatar]. Outcome: [X result in Y weeks without Z]. Give me: a headline, subhead, 5 bullet points, and a CTA button. Model from the IFCA LP examples in the project files.
Step 3 · 14-Day Content CalendarBuild my 14-day pre-launch content calendar for my [topic] masterclass. Include: 3 organic reels (with hooks), 2 carousel concepts, 3 story sequences, and 2 paid ad scripts. My avatar is [describe]. My mechanism is called [name].
Step 3 · Ad Hook BankGive me 15 ad hook options for my masterclass. Include: 5 POV hooks, 5 problem/villain hooks, and 5 call-out hooks ("Attention [avatar]..."). My avatar is [describe]. Pull from the paid ad creative examples in the project files.
Step 4–5 — Projections & Presentation
Step 4 · KPI ProjectionsSet my KPI projections for a [$X] ad spend campaign. My offer price is [$Y]. Based on my past campaign data in the project files, what are realistic CPL, show rate, booking rate, close rate, and ROAS targets? Which phase am I in?
Step 5 · 3-Secrets FrameworkHelp me build my 3-Secrets slide framework. My mechanism is called [name]. The 3 false beliefs I need to destroy are: (1) [belief], (2) [belief], (3) [belief]. Write the slide content and speaker script for each secret using the format in the project files.
Step 5 · Pitch ScriptWrite my no-pressure invitation pitch for the end of my masterclass. My call-to-action is a free [strategy call / assessment call / 30-min consult]. My offer is [name] at [$price]. Use Jeremy Haynes' 3-Pillar Pre-Sell Framework from the project files.
Step 5 · Confirmation Page VideoWrite my confirmation page video script. My masterclass topic is [topic]. My 3 client case studies are: [client 1 result], [client 2 result], [client 3 result]. My unique mechanism is [name]. Use the IFCA confirmation page video framework from the project files.
Step 6–7 — Hosting & Follow-Up
Step 7 · Follow-Up SequenceWrite my 7-touch post-masterclass follow-up sequence. I need: email + SMS for (1) attendees who didn't book, (2) no-shows, and (3) people who booked but haven't closed. My re-run is on Sunday. My offer is [name] at [$price]. Use the IFCA automation sequence templates.
Funnel DiagnosisDiagnose my funnel. My last campaign results were: ad spend [$X], leads [N], registrants [N], attendees [N], calls booked [N], closed [N], revenue [$X]. Show rate was [X]%, booking rate [X]%, close rate [X]%, ROAS [X]×. What is my #1 constraint and what's the specific fix?

Show Rate FixMy show rate is [X]%. My current nurture sequence is [describe or paste it]. Give me a rewritten 5-touch pre-masterclass nurture sequence (email + SMS) designed to get show rate above 30%. Pull from the IFCA automation templates in the project files.
Retargeting StrategyBuild me a re-run strategy for my masterclass. I had [N] registrants who didn't show. My live masterclass was on [day]. Walk me through: the re-run announcement, Sunday timing, follow-up sequence, and how to pitch it without devaluing the original event.

Based on my copy kit and business context in the project files, what are the top 3 positioning changes I should make before my next campaign?
